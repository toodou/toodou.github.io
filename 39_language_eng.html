Learning Language

As most people, my first foreign language is English because we need to learn in school. However, do you think you can speak in English wherever you are? The truth is impossible. Many regions like south of america or north asia, people who prefer or only can to speak in single language and this is not English. How can we do? At this moment, I prefer to define this moment as motivation for you to learn a new one! Sometimes, we just want to learn a new one becasue it might to be a little interesting. Maybe, we want to comprehend what the literatures are talking about. No matter what the reasons are, let's learn a new foreign language ~ ~ ~
