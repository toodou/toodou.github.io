Experiences

There are two parts in Experiences, one is Academic Experieces and Work Experiences.

Academic Experiences

For me, no matter in my experience or life, what I have done is "just do it" and "overcome my limitation"; then, you can have different thoughts and ideas while you do a new project or improve something that you have ever known before. Although several challenges and obstacles always exist there, you do not do it! You never know what kind of challenges is waiting for you!

My undergraduate project life 1 - Geography:
When I was searching what kind of research topic I wanted to do, and I thought that I wanted to help people, especially people who were suffered from landslide and debris-flow. This issue is very important in Taiwan caused geographical and meteorological characteristics. Prof. Yuan-Fan, Tsai has taught lots of methods to help me to solve research problems. At that time, I adopted the genetic algorithm, k-means clustering and support vector machine to figure out that could define critical rainfall line in the mountainous area.

My undergraduate project life 2 - Chemistry:
In my childhood, I was curious about the chemistry-related topic. I was very fortunate that have a chance to do a complete project in JTC Lab (Prof. Jwu-Ting, Chen, Dept. of Chemistry at National Taiwan University). My research topic is to synthesized several organometallic complexes as the catalyst to do bio-degradable plastic: from ligand to complex and polymerization. I have learned a lot no matter about synthesis or analysis.

A large challenge - seismic life in my graduate program
I fortunately and successfully entered into Dept. of Geography at National Taiwan University. I chose a way to continue disaster-related topic - earthquake! I have to say that was a big challenge under knowledge-less and skill-less background. But, fortunately, my advisors Prof. Tzu-How Chu and Prof. Jiun-Huei Proty Wu gave me this chance to learn signal processing and knowledge about electromagnetic signal; in addition, we combine time and frequency signal to figure out the seismic precursors.

A hard and black future - aviation life in my Ph.D. program
Due to the equipment problems, I have changed my dissertation topic to aviation market research, especially about the airline alliance airport network. As an airline fan, I was so lucky that I finally can do some research on aviation. What I have done was to utilize hierarchical community detection to retrieve the market segments of the airport network.

If you are curious about data science, please feel free to contact me!

Work Experiences

Because of my life plan, all works are related to research, but the types of works are diverse. Besides, I think there are two things that I want to tell you: being teaching assistant at the university and being an engineer!

About being a teaching assistant at the university
A teaching assistant is a job which needs to be the bridge between teacher and students; however, this is not what you think! You need to pay attention to your students. You may know that they don't understand what the teacher is talking, but you can let them know what it is; then you will become a good assistant. When I was a teaching assistant in the Russian course, I finally found out why students can't quickly grasp the key points or tips because they use learning experiences in other languages. Therefore, you can't learn very well because there is a shadow around you!

About being an engineer
I think people always have a lot of stereotypes, but for me, this job, as an engineer to teach me, you need to be ambitious about your work or project, because your coding potential and skills will directly affect your quality of work and life. So, you need to remember that improving your ability is the most important!
