Layer

All data have been classified into nine parts by their regions and usages, including Taiwan, Japan, USA, Russia, China, Global, map editor, web-based map and other planets, etc.

In this part, I spent a lot of time to collect these databases and divide them into different countries and types. Most of them are the shapefile format (available in ArcMap). In addition, part of them are spreadsheet format without login, however, some database requires account and application by yourself. If you have any question, please feel free to let me know! Thank you!
