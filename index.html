Chun-Hsiang Chan Ph.D.

A Spatiotemporal Data Scientist Skilled With Interdisciplinary Experience & Multidisciplinary Expertise

He is a Assistant Professor at Department of Geography, National Taiwan Normal University. He received a Ph.D. Department of Geography, National Taiwan University. Research interests focus on applying the SDGs to human dynamics. He applied artificial intelligence technology to spatiotemporal AI (urban mobility generation), cyber security AI (network attack detection in encrypted traffic), and medical AI (clinical research related to osteoporosis and cardiovascular calcification). Experienced data analyst with a history of working in academia and the information industry. To date, I have 16 academic/industrial research projects (PI and Co-PI), review 24 international journals, and 1 journal editorial board, including geographic science, medicine (e.g., cardiology and orthopedics), and food science. Some of them are top-ranked impact factor, e.g., European Heart Journal (IF: 37.6; 2/220) and Food Hydrocolliods (IF: 11.0; 3/173). Currently, published 12 journal articles (10 SCI/SSCI, 1 TSSCI, 1 EI), 54 conference papers, 1 book chapter, a co-authored book in Russian (Герои нашего времени из Тайваня в Россию и постсоветское пространство).

Highlights

Research

His research originated in "helping people." His research ranges from disaster prevention (mudslides and earthquakes) to disease prevention (infectious diseases, metabolic syndrome, and osteoporosis), requiring multidisciplinary knowledge to overcome research gaps and foster new solutions beyond conventional thinking. As a result, he has acquired expertise in various fields, with different perspectives and methods to achieve his goals.

Teaching

He leverages his education expertise in teaching. Three pivot theories elaborate his teaching philosophy: Scaffolding Theory from Лев Семёнович Выготский, Learning by Doing from John Deway, and Episodic Memory from Endel Tulving. In addition, he taught Advanced C Programming and It's Applications at Dept. of AI, Tamkang University, and had a wide teaching assistant experience in 16 courses among 6 departments and 4 universities.

Service

He serves as a reviewer for 25 journals (22 SCI; 1 EI; 2 Peer Review). Moreover, he collaborates with his friends as a Chief Data Scientist and Consultant at a Social Enterprise, Infinite Value, proffering services to maximize improvements in social and environmental well-being, ultimately achieving "triple win."

Experience

He devoted himself to the industry and went beyond academic limitations to gain expertise in related fields, such as Taiwan Cyber Security Foundry Company, Institute of Information Industry, and the National Science and Technology Center for Disaster Reduction.
